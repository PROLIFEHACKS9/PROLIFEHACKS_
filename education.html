Paperclips Hacks

As a student who is focused on learning, he is no stranger to paper clips. The question is, by using only paper clips, what can be done to save energy to buy goods and save costs. Equipped with a paperclip only. And only using 2-4 of each production we can produce a standphone or remote to replace a standphone or make a new compartment. First, we fold the existing paper clip from the length to the back and then remove the hook that holds the object that will be held there. After flapping. Try to balance each point balancing object and paper clip. It's ready! Good luck !
